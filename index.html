A Synthetic NFT Protocol


OctoMarket is a protocol which creates synthetic derivatives of NFTs, sort of like an NFT stable-coin. Synth NFTs are pegged to the floor price of their collection and similar other Defi protocols like MakerDAO are over-collateralized. Synth NFTs are open and fungible like any other ERC20.

The peg of synth NFTs are secured by a smart contract based oracle in which community operated bots submit price data from whitelisted sources, and arb bots stabilize the price across markets. This unique decentralized architecture protects against oracle attacks.

Synth NFTs Are OmniChain

Synth NFTs minted with OctoMarket are OmniChain at the core and are seamlessly transferable between blockchains without the need for a bridge. OctoMarket currently supports the 18+ networks on Axelar and any open AMM or DEX on those chains.


With a Synth NFT DEX


Synth NFTs can be traded on any DEX like Uniswap, but we also have a Decentralized Exchange which allows you to long or short Synth NFTs. Providing liquidity on the OctoMarket DEX also allows you to earn yield rewards.
