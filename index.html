Price Pegged

Synthetic Fungible Tokens are like stable coins which are pegged to a Collections floor price. So the price of BoredApeSFT on Uniswap is pegged to the Bored Ape floor price on OpenSea.

Omni-Chain

OctoMarkets SFTs are omni-chain, you can transfer them between 20 blockchains without a bridge. You can trade your Ethereum BoredApeSFT on BSC.

Extemely Fungible

You can trade SFTs on our DEX to earn max yield rewards, but you can also trade SFTs on other DEXs such as Uniswap, PancakeSwap, SushiSwap, etc.


Fungible Liquid NFTs


OctoMarket is a smart contract based protocol which creates synthetic derivatives of top NFTs called Synthetic-Fungible Tokens. You can think of them as NFT stable-coins. SFTs are pegged to the floor price of their collection, so a single BoredApeSFT is worth the floor price of the Bored Ape collection.

OctoMarket SFTs are also Omnichain. Using the Axelar Network they can be transfered between blockchain networks without any need for a bridge. You never need to buy the NFT to trade an SFT, and you can trade any amount.

Earn Yield Minting SFTs


All Synthetic Fungible Tokens are backed by collateral supplied by the community, similar to other DeFi protocols like MakerDAO. You can earn rewards in several ways: by minting new SFTs, by supplying liquidity to an AMM, and we offer yield rewards for staking your LP tokens.
